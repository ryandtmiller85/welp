## What to Test

Explore the site naturally, as if you just went through a breakup and a friend sent you this link. Here's what to try:

### 1. Sign Up / Sign In
- Log in with your test credentials
- Try the "Forgot password?" flow
- After logging in, does the header show "Dashboard"?
- Log out — does the header go back to "Log In"?

### 2. Set Up Your Registry
- From the dashboard, click **Edit Profile**
- Give yourself a display name and write a short story
- Set your privacy level (try "Public" so others can see it)
- Add items to your registry:
  - Try pasting an Amazon URL to auto-import a product
  - Try adding something manually
  - Try the curated catalog

### 3. Create a Cash Fund
- From the dashboard, create a new fund
- Give it a title, goal amount, and description
- Check that it shows on your public registry page

### 4. Visit Someone Else's Registry
- Go to the **Browse** page
- Find another tester's public registry
- Try leaving an encouragement message
- Try clicking "Contribute" on a fund (you can go up to the Stripe page but **don't enter real payment info**)

### 5. Create a Registry for a Friend
- Go to `/create-for`
- Pretend you're setting up a registry for a friend going through a breakup
- Walk through the steps

### 6. Explore the Site
- Check out the **Shop** and **Merch** pages
- Read the **About** page
- Check the **Terms**, **Privacy**, and **Community Guidelines** pages (footer links)
- Try browsing on your phone (or resize your browser window)

## How to Give Feedback

There's a **pink button** in the bottom-right corner of every page. Click it to open the feedback form.

### Steps:
1. Click the pink 💬 button
2. Select a category: **Bug**, **Confusing**, **Suggestion**, **Love it**, or **General**
3. Describe what you noticed
4. (Optional) Click **"Capture & annotate screen"** to take a screenshot and draw red rectangles around problem areas
5. Click **Submit Feedback**

### What to Report:
- **Bugs** — Something broke or didn't work as expected
- **Confusing** — You weren't sure what to do or what something meant
- **Suggestion** — An idea that would make things better
- **Love it** — Something that felt great (we want to know what works too!)
- **General** — Anything else

### Be Specific!
Instead of: *"The page is weird"*
Try: *"After adding an item via URL, the price shows as $0.00 even though the original page says $29.99"*
